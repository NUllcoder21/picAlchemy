How to Use PicAlchemy

Step 1: Upload Your Image

Click the "Choose File" button and select the image you want to edit from your device.

Step 2: Choose an Editing Operation

Select the desired editing operation from the dropdown menu:

  • Convert to PNG - Change your image format to PNG.
  • Convert to Gray - Convert your image to grayscale.
  • Convert to WebP - Convert your image to WebP format.
  • Convert to JPG - Change your image format to JPG, etc.

Step 3: Submit Your Image

Click the "Submit" button to process your image. If you are not logged in, you will be redirected to the login page.

Step 4: Download Your Edited Image

Once the image is processed, a download button will appear. Click it to download your edited image.
